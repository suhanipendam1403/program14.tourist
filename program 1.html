Maharashtra's tourism events blend culture, adventure, and nature, featuring major festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi, unique experiences such as the Eco Glamping Festival in Nashik (Jan-Mar 2025), and large-scale celebrations like the Maha Mega Tourism Festival in Mahabaleshwar (May 2025) with drone shows, cultural parades, and adventure sports. Key highlights include spiritual festivals, agro-tourism, and promotional roadshows by the state tourism department to promote diverse offerings like trekking, water sports, and heritage sites. Major Festivals & Events Ganesh Chaturthi: The most significant festival, celebrated with grand processions and cultural vibrancy, especially around Mahabaleshwar. Eco Glamping Festival (Nashik): January-March 2025, offering luxury camping, cultural activities, and nature experiences near Gangapur Dam. Maharashtra Mega Tourism Festival (Mahabaleshwar): Features Lavani, folk performances, drone/laser shows, floating markets, and adventure activities like paragliding. Gudi Padwa: Maharashtra's traditional harvest festival, marking the New Year. Lavasa Women's Drive (March): A unique event promoting women empowerment, scenic drives, and awareness. Adventure & Nature Adventure Tourism: Opportunities for trekking, rafting, caving, rock climbing, and camping are promoted across the state. Agro-Tourism: Centers like those in Latur offer farm stays and rural experiences. Water Sports: Available in various locations, including Mumbai's beaches and lakes. Cultural & Promotional Events Departmental Events: Maharashtra Tourism organizes promotional roadshows and festivals (e.g., World Tourism Day) to highlight destinations and new initiatives. Folk Performances: Enjoy traditional arts like Lavani, Gondhal, and Jagar at various festivals. How to Find More Info Maharashtra Tourism Website: Maharashtra Tourism for official festival listings. Utsav.gov.in: Lists upcoming and past events like the Eco Glamping Festival. MahaBooking: For agro-tourism and staycation options.
